Welcome to a simple to-do list

Here you are able to add any task in order to remember all yours tasks
Once you have completed the task, click on 'Complete' - this will move it to the completed list
If you wish to delete an task, just click the dropdown menu on the task and click 'Delete'

This to-do list will remember your tasks for this browser, so dont be afraid to close the tab & come back later :)
